How to Delete Your Smart Food Account

Smart Food respects your privacy and gives you full control over your account and personal data. You can request account deletion at any time.

Option 1: Delete Account from the App

  • Open the Smart Food app
  • Go to Profile → Settings
  • Select Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion request

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you may request account deletion by sending an email from your registered email address to:

Suffix Digital - Mobile App

Please include the subject line: “Delete My Smart Food Account”

What Data Is Deleted

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User authentication data
  • Profile and account details
  • Order history and related records

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. Some data may be retained temporarily if required by law.

Backend & Security

Smart Food uses Supabase for authentication and database services. Account deletion removes user records from Supabase systems securely.
