Privacy Policy – Smart Food

1. Introduction

Smart Food ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Smart Food mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User ID and authentication metadata
  • Device information (OS version, app version)
  • Usage data related to app features

3. Authentication & Backend Services

Smart Food uses Supabase for:

  • Email & password authentication
  • Phone number authentication (OTP)
  • Social login (Google and other providers)
  • Secure database storage

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate users securely
  • To manage user accounts
  • To enable food browsing and ordering features
  • To improve app performance and stability

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. Data is shared only with trusted infrastructure providers such as Supabase for core app functionality.

6. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Access to data is restricted and protected using industry-standard security practices.

7. Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion directly within the app or by contacting us via email. Upon deletion, all personal data associated with the account is permanently removed from our systems, except where required by law.

8. Children’s Privacy

Smart Food is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
